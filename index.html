Research

Higgs Boson Pair Production Search

Led analysis of the two light lepton + tau channel in a nine-channel search for non-resonant Higgs boson pair production using 140 fb⁻¹ of proton-proton collision data.

Developed machine learning classifiers including boosted decision trees and deep neural networks to separate signal from background in extremely low signal-to-background environments.

Engineered more than 100 kinematic variables and optimized feature selection for multivariate analysis.
